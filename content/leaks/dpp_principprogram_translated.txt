The Danish Party

Draft Manifesto for 2024

CONFIDENTIAL

The purpose of the Danish Party is to secure Havland's progress and Danish cooperation in culture, trade, and security. This manifesto will be the foundation for the Danish Party's political position.

1. Approve and carry out the Language Council's decision for the modernising of the Havlandic language.
2. An official vote on Havlandic independence after 10 years.
3. Pass and carry out the Community Security Act.
4. Modernising of the government and government communication for working from home due to COVID 19.
5. (2018 manifesto here)

For modernisation and progress.
